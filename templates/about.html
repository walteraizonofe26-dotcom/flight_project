About FlySafe

Your trusted airline for comfortable and affordable travel across Africa

Our Story

FlySafe was founded in 2013 with a vision to provide affordable, reliable, and comfortable air travel across Africa. We started with a small fleet and a big dream to connect people across the continent with safe, punctual, and affordable flights.

Today, we operate a modern fleet of aircraft serving major destinations across Nigeria, Ghana, Rwanda, Uganda, Kenya, and other African countries. Our commitment to safety, comfort, and customer service has made us one of the most trusted airlines in the region.

Our Mission

To provide safe, reliable, and affordable air transportation that connects people and communities across Africa, while maintaining the highest standards of safety and customer service.

Our Vision

To be the leading airline in Africa, known for excellence in service, innovation, and sustainable growth that benefits our customers, employees, and communities we serve.
